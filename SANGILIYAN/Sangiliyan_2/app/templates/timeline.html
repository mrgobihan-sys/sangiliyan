TIMELINE

The Epic Journey of the Jaffna Kingdom

1215

The Foundation

Magha of Kalinga establishes the independent Kingdom of Jaffna, securing the north and beginning the Aryacakravarti dynasty.

1350

Maritime Power

The kingdom reaches its peak, controlling the pearl trade and becoming a dominant naval force in the Indian Ocean.

1450

Sapumal's Invasion

The Kingdom of Kotte briefly occupies Jaffna under Sapumal Kumaraya, but independence is reclaimed shortly after.

1505

Portuguese Arrival

European explorers arrive on the island, changing the geopolitical landscape of the region forever.

1560

The Defense of Mannar

King Cankili I leads a fierce resistance against Portuguese incursions and protects the local traditions.

1617 - 1619

Reign of Sangiliyan II

The last King of Jaffna leads a heroic last stand against colonial forces to protect the city of Nallur.

1619

Fall of Nallur

After a decisive and bloody battle, the kingdom is annexed by the Portuguese, ending centuries of independent rule.
